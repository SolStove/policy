SolStove Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 17th, 2026

SolStove is a household-facing app that helps users connect to their stove’s data logger, sync cooking and device data, and view information related to their SolStove use.

Information We Collect

Household access information

The app may ask for a SolStove household code and 4-digit PIN to bind the app to a household. When binding succeeds, the app receives a household access token and logger identifier. These are stored locally on the device so the app can access the correct household data.

Stove, logger, and cooking data

When a user chooses to sync their stove, the app uses Bluetooth to connect to the household’s SolStove data logger and retrieve stove or logger data. This may include logger identifiers, cooking records, energy or usage-related records, timestamps or sequence information, sync status, dataset metadata, device health information, and storage integrity information.

Diagnostics

The app may collect diagnostics such as app version, build environment, operating system, operating system version, device model, locale, session identifier, crash information, error information, network events, Bluetooth events, sync events, command events, device-health events, diagnostic breadcrumbs, and the logger identifier associated with the bound household.

Bluetooth and location-related permissions

The app uses Bluetooth to scan for and connect to the household’s SolStove data logger. On some Android versions, Bluetooth scanning requires location permission from the operating system. SolStove does not use Bluetooth or location-related permissions to track a user’s physical location for advertising, profiling, or cross-app tracking.

Network information

The app communicates with SolStove servers to bind a household, verify household access, sync or upload stove/logger data, retrieve household data, and upload diagnostics. Standard server logs may include technical information such as IP address, request time, endpoint, response status, and security or reliability metadata.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not use the app to collect payment information, contact lists, photos, messages, camera data, microphone data, or advertising identifiers. We do not use the app to serve ads.

How We Use Information

We use information to bind the app to the correct household, authenticate household access, connect to SolStove data loggers, retrieve, store, sync, and display stove/logger data, provide household-facing features, diagnose app or device issues, improve reliability and security, provide support, prevent data loss, and comply with legal or operational obligations.

How Information Is Shared

Information may be shared with SolStove systems and service providers that help us operate, host, secure, store, or troubleshoot the service. We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, security obligations, or a business transfer.

We do not sell data. We do not share data with advertising networks or data brokers. We do not use collected data for third-party advertising, advertising measurement, or tracking across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Data Storage and Retention

The app stores certain information locally, including the household access token, household logger identifier, diagnostics queue, synced stove/logger data, and related metadata. SolStove retains uploaded household, stove, logger, and diagnostic data for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, provide household features, support troubleshooting, maintain security, meet legal obligations, and preserve data integrity.

User Choices

Users may clear or switch the bound household if available in the app, decline permissions, delete the app, or clear app data through the operating system. Some features may not work without Bluetooth or related operating-system permissions.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information handled by the app. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

SolStove is intended for household use in connection with SolStove products and services. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app.

International Processing

Information may be processed in countries where SolStove or its service providers operate. These countries may have data protection laws different from those in the user’s location.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and provide notice as required by applicable law or app-store policies.
