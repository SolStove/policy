SolStove Operator Privacy Policy

Effective Date:June 17th, 2026

SolStove Operator is a field-operations app used by authorized SolStove technicians to connect to SolStove data loggers, retrieve device data, upload operational datasets, manage firmware-related tasks, and diagnose app or device issues.

Information We Collect

Operator authentication

The app uses an operator access token provided to an authorized technician. The token is used to verify access to SolStove services and authenticate cloud requests. The token is stored locally on the device until the technician signs out or clears app data.

Logger and operational data

When a technician connects to a SolStove data logger, the app may retrieve and store logger identifiers, raw data files, dataset metadata, record counts, sequence information, device health information, firmware status, and sync status.

Firmware and imported files

The app allows authorized technicians to import or download firmware files, firmware manifests, raw data files, or ZIP files for operational purposes. The app only accesses files selected by the technician through the device file picker.

Diagnostics

The app may collect diagnostics such as app version, build environment, operating system, operating system version, device model, locale, session identifier, crash information, error information, network events, Bluetooth events, firmware update events, sync events, command events, device-health events, diagnostic breadcrumbs, and the logger identifier associated with an active service session.

Bluetooth and location-related permissions

The app uses Bluetooth to scan for and connect to SolStove data loggers. On some Android versions, Bluetooth scanning requires location permission from the operating system. SolStove Operator does not use Bluetooth or location-related permissions to track a technician’s physical location for advertising, profiling, or cross-app tracking.

Network information

The app communicates with SolStove servers to verify operator tokens, upload datasets, download firmware, check sync state, fetch batch status, and upload diagnostics. Standard server logs may include technical information such as IP address, request time, endpoint, response status, and security or reliability metadata.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not use the app to collect payment information, contact lists, photos, messages, camera data, microphone data, or advertising identifiers. We do not use the app to serve ads.

How We Use Information

We use information to authenticate technicians, connect to data loggers, retrieve and store logger data, upload operational datasets, manage firmware, support over-the-air firmware updates, diagnose issues, maintain security and reliability, provide field support, and comply with legal or operational obligations.

How Information Is Shared

Information may be shared with SolStove systems and service providers that help us operate, host, secure, store, or troubleshoot the service. We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, security obligations, or a business transfer.

We do not sell data. We do not share data with advertising networks or data brokers. We do not use collected data for third-party advertising, advertising measurement, or tracking across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Data Storage and Retention

The app stores certain information locally, including the operator token, diagnostics queue, firmware files, raw data files, sync ledgers, and related metadata. SolStove retains uploaded operational data and diagnostics for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, support field operations, diagnose issues, maintain security, meet legal obligations, and preserve data integrity.

User Choices

Technicians may sign out to remove the stored operator token, decline permissions, delete the app, or clear app data through the operating system. Some features may not work without Bluetooth or related operating-system permissions.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information handled by the app. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

SolStove Operator is intended for authorized SolStove technicians and field staff. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app.

International Processing

Information may be processed in countries where SolStove or its service providers operate. These countries may have data protection laws different from those in the user’s location.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and provide notice as required by applicable law or app-store policies.
